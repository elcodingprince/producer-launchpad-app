# Producer Launchpad Merchant Data Processing Terms

Draft status: internal working draft for review and finalization.

## Purpose

This document summarizes how Producer Launchpad processes merchant and customer data to operate the app for merchants.

## Role

Producer Launchpad processes customer and order-related data only as needed to provide the app's functionality to the merchant, including license generation, digital delivery, delivery recovery, and compliance handling.

## Categories Of Data Processed

Producer Launchpad may process:

- merchant store identifiers and configuration
- customer name
- customer email
- Shopify order identifiers and order details
- order line item licensing details
- generated agreement and delivery records
- limited technical and audit metadata related to the transaction

## Permitted Purposes

Producer Launchpad processes this data only to:

- associate purchases with buyers
- identify the licensee on generated license agreements
- generate agreement evidence
- create and manage secure delivery access
- send and track delivery emails
- support merchants in re-delivery or support workflows
- fulfill Shopify and privacy law compliance obligations

## Restrictions

Producer Launchpad will not use merchant customer data for:

- advertising
- cross-merchant profiling
- unrelated analytics
- unrelated product personalization

## Retention

Producer Launchpad should retain customer data only for the limited periods described in the internal data retention policy and only for the purposes listed above.

## Security Expectations

Producer Launchpad should operate with:

- encrypted transport
- encrypted production storage
- encrypted backups
- separated test and production environments
- least-privilege staff access
- access logging for sensitive operations
- an incident response process

## Merchant Responsibilities

The merchant remains responsible for:

- lawfully selling and delivering their content
- ensuring they have rights to the content and terms they offer
- configuring accurate storefront and support information
- responding to any end-customer obligations they control directly

## Privacy Requests

Producer Launchpad records Shopify privacy request events and supports operational fulfillment for:

- customer data requests
- customer redaction
- shop redaction

## Finalization Note

Before public launch, this draft should be turned into final merchant-facing terms or a data processing section that is linked in the app listing and any applicable merchant documentation.
