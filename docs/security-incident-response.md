# Producer Launchpad Security Incident Response Policy

Draft status: internal working draft.

## Purpose

This document defines a minimum response process for security incidents affecting Producer Launchpad systems or protected customer data.

## Incident Examples

- unauthorized access to production systems
- exposure of protected customer data
- leaked credentials
- accidental sharing of privacy exports
- compromise of email, storage, or database infrastructure

## Response Process

### 1. Identify

- confirm the incident
- capture the affected systems, users, and data categories
- preserve relevant logs and evidence

### 2. Contain

- restrict or disable affected accounts
- revoke or rotate credentials
- isolate affected systems if needed

### 3. Assess

- determine what data may have been affected
- determine whether merchants or Shopify need notification
- assess whether delivery, agreement, or privacy request workflows were impacted

### 4. Remediate

- fix the underlying issue
- restore systems safely
- confirm access controls and secrets are corrected

### 5. Communicate

- notify internal owners
- notify affected merchants where required
- use `newradio.sound@gmail.com` as the current support and privacy contact until a domain-based support inbox is in place
- document actions taken and follow-up work

### 6. Review

- record the root cause
- identify prevention steps
- update policies or controls if needed

## Minimum Preparedness Requirements

- maintain current access to logs and provider consoles
- know who owns production infrastructure decisions
- keep provider credentials ready for emergency rotation
- keep merchant support contact information current before public launch
